# TrustGrid Compliance Templates

Open-source compliance policy templates for fintech, digital lending, and ecommerce businesses.

Maintained by [Olamide Mohammed](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olamidelatifatm) — Founder, TrustGrid Technology Limited.

## Templates Available

| Template | Description | Framework |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| [GDPR Privacy Policy](./gdpr-privacy-policy.md) | Privacy policy for EU-facing digital products | GDPR |
| [NDPR Privacy Notice](./ndpr-privacy-notice.md) | Privacy notice for Nigerian businesses | NDPR 2019 |
| [KYC Policy](./kyc-policy.md) | Know Your Customer policy for fintech/lending apps | CBN/FATF |
| [AML Policy](./aml-policy.md) | Anti-Money Laundering policy framework | FATF/CBN |
| [Incident Response Plan](./incident-response-plan.md) | Security incident response procedures | ISO 27001 |
| [Data Retention Policy](./data-retention-policy.md) | Data retention and deletion framework | GDPR/NDPR |

## Usage

These templates are starting points. Every business should customise them to reflect their actual data practices, jurisdiction, and product specifics before use.
